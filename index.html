 FAQ

Q1. What is SME Mobile App?

SME Mobile is an application running on Smart Phone that has internet access, enabling customers to perform banking transactions conveniently, securely from anywhere, anytime. You can activate your existing SME bank account or open a new account easily.

Q2. Why should you choose to use SME mobile?

SME Mobile provides convenient and fast way of banking transactions with commonly use functions such as Fund Transfer, Mobile Tup Up, Bill Payment, Account Management, etc.

Q3. What are the functions of SME Mobile?

  • Account Management
    • View balance and activities in your accounts
    • Download account statement.
    • Set profile picture and view profile information.
    • Set your prefer language to Khmer, or English
  • Open new Saving Account
  • Open new Fixed Deposit Account
  • Apply Loan
  • KHQR
    • Scan QR
    • Generate QR
  • Money Transfer
    • Transfer between own accounts
    • Transfer to other SME accounts
    • Local Bank transfer via Bakong
  • Mobile Top Up
  • Bill Payment
  • Manage Favorite Transfer and Payment
  • Schedule Transfer and Payment
  • Products & Services
  • Locate Us (Branch & ATM)
  • Security Setting
  • FAQs

Q4. What are the requirements to use SME Mobile?

  • Having a mobile phone running Android OS v12 or higher / Apple iOS v15 or higher.
  • With internet access, download and install the SME Mobile App from Play Store or App Store.

Q5. How to activate SME Mobile?

If you already have account(s) with SME Bank of Cambodia, you need to:

  • Follow the instructions to Activate Mobile by providing the required information.
  • Have the valid mobile number that you registered with your account.
  • Agree to terms and conditions!

Q6. How to open a new account using SME Mobile?

If you do not have SME Bank account yet, please follow the below requirements:

  • Follow the instructions to Open New Account by providing the required information.
  • Age from 18 years old.
  • Having valid phone number (local)
  • Having Cambodia ID card
  • Agreed to terms and conditions!

Q7. How many devices can I connect to SME Mobile?

Based on security reasons, you can connect only one device with SME Mobile.

Q8. What will happen if I change my device?

Just log in to the account on your new device. The information in your previous device will log out immediately.

Q9. What should I do if I lost my mobile phone?

Your SME Mobile is still safe and secure as it needs a PIN to authorize login. To block your account, please visit the nearest SME Bank branches or contact our Care Center number: 096 8111 118.

Q10. What is OTP code?

A one-time password (OTP) is a password which is valid for only one login session or transaction.

Q11. What if I have not received OTP code via SMS?

Please try these simple steps:

  • Clear old message data.
  • Refresh the phone's service by turning off airplane mode then turning it on.
  • Trying on p “Resend” after refreshing phone's service.
  • Find a better place for phone service.

You can visit the nearest SME bank's branches or contact Care Center number: 096 8111 118.

Q12. What should I do if I forgot my PIN/Password?

Please click on the "Forgot PIN" menu, then follow the instructions on SME Mobile App. Please click on the "Forgot Password" menu, then follow the instructions on SME Mobile App. You can visit the nearest SME Bank's branches or contact Care Centre number: 096 8111 118.

Q13. Need help & support?

If your need more information or assistance, please visit the nearest SME Bank’s branches or contact to our Care Center number: 096 8111 118.
